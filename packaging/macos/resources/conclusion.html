Next Steps

  1. Look for the terminal icon in your menu bar (top-right, near WiFi/battery)
  2. Click the icon to open the dropdown menu — you'll see a status indicator showing your certificate state
  3. Select "Generate / Renew Cert" to authenticate
  4. Complete the SSO login in your browser
  5. The status will show green when your certificate is active
Your SSH certificates are valid for 12 hours. The menu bar will remind you to renew before expiration.
